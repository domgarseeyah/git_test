Basic Lasagna Recipe

Recipe Ingredients

  • 1 pound of Ground Beef
  • 12 ounces of Whole Leafed Spinich
  • One box of lasagna-style pasta.
    (Can be parboiled)
  • One large jar of red marinara sauce
  • One bag of mixed mac-and-cheese blended cheese

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees fahenheit
  2. Begin to boil water and place noodles in to partially cook
  3. Brown ground beef in a medium sized pan. Drain excess liquid and set aside to cool slightly before use
  4. Get oven-pan ready: Spray to avoid food from sticking
  5. Begin Layering: meat, sauce, cheese, pasta, repeat!
  6. Finish of with a little cheese and cover to prevent pre-mature crisping

Finishing touches:

  • Carefully Place in Oven covered for 20 min
  • Take out and remove covering, finish baking. (Apporx. 10min)
  • Let Cool, portion with a knife or large spoon and enjoy!
