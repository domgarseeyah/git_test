Pulled Pork Recipe

On a lazy weekend day. There is always time to put together a dinner that has the family asking for more!

What you'll need!

  • One boneless pork roast
  • A crock pot
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • Three cloves of garlic minced
  • One 12oz can of rootbeer
  • A barbeque sauce of your choice
  • A set of 12 bulkie rolls

Cooking

  1. Trim pork of excess fat/skin
  2. Place in crockpot, lightly season if desired
  3. Spread brownsugar evenly on the pork
  4. Pour rootbeer over pork
  5. Lightly pour bbq sauce on meat
  6. Set heat to low and let sit for 4 hours
  7. Check on liquid levels, flip if top is uncovered, cook for another hour
  8. Remove pork from pot, allow to slightly cool before pulling
  9. As the pork is pulled, place back into crockpot and let sit for 10 min
  10. Serve on a roll or from straight from the bowl! (Add bbq sauce if desired)
